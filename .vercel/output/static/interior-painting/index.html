Frequently Asked Questions - Interior Painting

How long does interior painting take?

Most interior painting projects take 2-4 days for an average-sized home. The timeline depends on the size of the space, number of rooms, and amount of prep work required. We provide detailed timelines with every estimate.

Do I need to move furniture before painting?

We can help move furniture to the center of rooms and cover it properly. However, we recommend removing small valuables, wall decorations, and delicate items beforehand. We'll take care of protecting your floors and larger furniture.

What type of paint do you use for interior work?

We use premium brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore for interior projects. These paints offer excellent coverage, durability, and are low-VOC for healthier indoor air quality. We can recommend specific paint types based on each room's needs.
