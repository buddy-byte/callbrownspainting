Frequently Asked Questions - Emergency Painting

Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency response for urgent painting needs related to water damage, smoke damage, or storm damage. Call us at (251) 237-4545 for immediate assistance throughout South Alabama.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we regularly work with insurance companies and can provide detailed documentation, photos, and estimates for insurance claims. We'll work directly with your adjuster to ensure proper coverage for your painting repairs.

How quickly can you respond?

For emergency situations, we typically respond within 24-48 hours. Time is critical with water and smoke damage, so we prioritize emergency calls to prevent further damage and begin restoration as quickly as possible.
