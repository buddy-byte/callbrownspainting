Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our painting services, pricing, and processes

📋 General Questions

How much does house painting cost in South Alabama?

Painting costs typically range from $2.50 to $4.50 per square foot for interior work and $1.80 to $3.50 per square foot for exterior painting. The final price depends on factors like the size of your home, surface preparation needed, paint quality, and project complexity. We offer free, detailed estimates for all South Alabama properties.

How long does it take to paint a house?

Most residential painting projects take 3-7 days to complete. Interior painting typically takes 2-4 days for an average-sized home, while exterior painting usually requires 3-5 days depending on weather conditions. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your free estimate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Brown's Painting is fully licensed and insured to work throughout South Alabama including Mobile, Baldwin, and Escambia counties. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage, protecting both our team and your property. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

Do I need to be home during the painting?

Not necessarily. Many homeowners prefer to be away during painting to avoid the inconvenience. We're fully insured and trustworthy, and can work independently once access is arranged. For interior projects, some homeowners prefer to be present initially, but it's entirely your choice.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of South Alabama including Mobile, Daphne, Fairhope, Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Foley, Bay Minette, Atmore, Brewton, and surrounding communities in Mobile County, Baldwin County, and Escambia County.

🎨 Paint & Materials

What brands of paint do you use?

We primarily use premium brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. These professional-grade paints offer superior coverage, durability, and color retention - essential for Alabama's humid climate. We can also accommodate specific brand preferences if you have particular requirements.

Do you use low-VOC or eco-friendly paints?

Yes! We offer low-VOC and zero-VOC paint options for homeowners concerned about indoor air quality. These paints are safer for families, pets, and the environment while still providing excellent coverage and durability.

What type of paint is best for Alabama's climate?

For exteriors, we recommend 100% acrylic latex paints with mildew resistance and UV protection. For interiors, we suggest moisture-resistant paints in high-humidity areas like bathrooms and kitchens. Our experience with Alabama's climate ensures we select the right products for long-lasting results.

Do you provide the paint or do I need to purchase it?

We provide all materials including paint, primer, and supplies as part of our service. This ensures we use professional-grade products and the correct quantities. However, if you've already purchased paint or have specific preferences, we're happy to work with your materials.

🔧 Process & Preparation

How do I prepare my home for painting?

For interior projects, we recommend removing small valuables, wall decorations, and delicate items. We'll move and cover furniture. For exterior painting, please ensure access to all sides of your home and remove items from porches and decks. We handle all other preparation including cleaning, repairs, and surface prep.

Do you move furniture?

Yes, we can help move furniture to the center of rooms and cover it properly. However, we recommend you move or store small valuables, electronics, and delicate items beforehand for their safety.

Is pressure washing included with exterior painting?

Yes! Pressure washing is included with all exterior painting projects. Proper surface cleaning is essential for paint adhesion and longevity. We thoroughly clean all surfaces before any painting begins.

Do you repair drywall and wood damage?

Absolutely. We provide complete drywall repair, patching, texture matching, and wood rot repair as part of our surface preparation. Proper repairs are crucial for a quality paint job that lasts.

What happens if it rains during exterior painting?

We monitor weather forecasts closely and only paint when we have adequate dry time. If unexpected rain occurs, we take immediate steps to protect your property. Modern paints typically need 2-4 hours to cure enough to withstand light rain, but we prefer 24-48 hours of clear weather for optimal results.

💰 Pricing & Estimates

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, detailed estimates with no obligation. We'll visit your property, assess the project scope, discuss your vision, and provide a written estimate that breaks down all costs with no hidden fees.

How quickly can I get an estimate?

We typically schedule estimate appointments within 1-2 business days and provide written estimates within 24 hours of the site visit. For urgent projects, we can often accommodate same-day or next-day estimates.

What affects the cost of a painting project?

Several factors influence pricing: square footage, ceiling height, surface condition, amount of prep work needed, number of colors, paint quality selected, and project complexity. We provide detailed estimates so you understand exactly what you're paying for.

Do you offer payment plans?

We work with each client individually to establish a payment schedule that works for their budget. Typically, we request a deposit to schedule your project, progress payments for larger jobs, and final payment upon completion and your satisfaction.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we regularly work with insurance companies for water damage, smoke damage, and storm-related painting repairs. We can provide detailed documentation, photos, and estimates for insurance claims and work directly with your adjuster.

📅 Scheduling & Timeline

How far in advance should I schedule?

We recommend scheduling 2-4 weeks in advance for most projects, especially during our busy season (spring and fall). However, we can often accommodate rush projects and always make room for emergency painting needs.

What's the best time of year for exterior painting in Alabama?

Spring and fall are ideal for exterior painting in Alabama, with mild temperatures and lower humidity. However, we can paint year-round by monitoring weather conditions and choosing appropriate days for optimal paint curing.

Can you paint in winter?

Yes, we can paint interiors year-round. For exteriors, we can paint during winter months when temperatures are above 50°F and conditions are dry. Modern paints have improved cold-weather performance, making winter painting more feasible than ever.

Do you work weekends or after hours?

For commercial projects, we offer flexible scheduling including evenings, weekends, and overnight shifts to minimize disruption to your business. For residential projects, we typically work weekdays but can accommodate special scheduling requests.

⭐ Special Services

Do you paint kitchen cabinets?

Yes! Cabinet refinishing is one of our specialties. We provide professional cabinet painting and refinishing with factory-quality results, transforming your kitchen at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Can you paint brick or stucco?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience painting brick, stucco, and other masonry surfaces. We use specialized primers and paints designed for these materials to ensure proper adhesion and long-lasting results.

Do you offer color consultation?

Yes! We provide expert color consultation to help you choose the perfect colors for your space. We consider factors like lighting, room size, architectural style, and your personal preferences to create a cohesive color palette.

Can you match existing paint colors?

Yes, we can match existing paint colors using color-matching technology. Bring us a paint chip or sample, and we can create an exact match for touch-ups or matching new areas to existing paint.

Do you paint commercial properties?

Yes, we provide comprehensive commercial painting services for offices, retail spaces, restaurants, and other commercial properties. We offer flexible scheduling to minimize disruption to your business operations.
